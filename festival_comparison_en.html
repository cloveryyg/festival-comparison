Spring Festival

Time: Lunar New Year
🏮👨‍👩‍👧‍👴

A traditional Chinese family celebration with red decorations, lanterns, and festive couplets. The inverted "福" (fú) character represents good fortune and blessings for the new year.

Christmas

Time: December 25th
🎄🎅

A Western holiday featuring decorated Christmas trees, Santa Claus, and gift exchanges. Celebrated with family gatherings and festive decorations.
